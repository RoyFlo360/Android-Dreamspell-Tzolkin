Privacy Policy

Dreamspell Calendar (com.imix.dreamspell_tzolkin)
Effective 17 July 2026 · Last updated 17 July 2026

Dreamspell Calendar does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no access to the internet and requests no permissions from your device.

Data we collect

None. We collect no personal or sensitive user data of any kind — no name, email address, account, contacts, photos, files, location, device identifiers, advertising ID, or usage analytics.

Why we cannot collect data, even accidentally

Dreamspell Calendar does not request the Android INTERNET permission. Without it, the Android operating system prevents the app from making any network connection whatsoever. The app therefore has no technical ability to send information anywhere. Every calendar calculation — the galactic signature, the Destiny Oracle, the Tzolkin grid, the wavespell, the 13-Moon calendar and the moon phase — is computed entirely on your device from content bundled inside the app.

Permissions

The app requests no Android permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, files, or storage.

Information stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of information locally on your device only. It is never transmitted, and it is removed when you uninstall the app.

WhatWhyWhere
Your chosen language So the app opens in the language you picked On-device app storage, via Android's standard per-app language setting
The date you are viewing So the screen survives rotation In memory only, for the current session
Kins added to the Kin Combinator So your selection persists while you use the screen In memory only, for the current session

Third parties

The app contains no advertising, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no third-party tracking software of any kind. No data is shared with anyone, because none is gathered.

Links that leave the app

The "Buy me a coffee" option opens buymeacoffee.com in your own web browser. Once you leave Dreamspell Calendar, this policy no longer applies and that website's privacy policy and terms govern your visit. We receive no personal information about you from it. Opening this link is entirely optional and nothing happens unless you tap it.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children under 13.

Your rights

Rights to access, correct, export, or erase personal data — such as those under the GDPR or CCPA — concern data a developer holds about you. We hold none. To remove everything the app has stored on your device, uninstall the app.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the revised version will be published at this address with an updated date above. Material changes will accompany an app update.
